Admissions

Bangira is a mixed school. The school offers education for students in forms 1 to 6 Bangira Academy is a center for June and November examination of Zimbabwe Schools Examination Council. Subject provided ENGLISH, SHONA, HISTORY , COMMERCIALS, GEOGRAPHY, MATHEMATICS, SCIENCES, COMPUTING, FRENCH, AGRICULTURE, ART, MUSIC, PHYSICAL EDUCATION SPORT, TECHNICAL GRAPHICS, WOOD TECHNOLOGY, METAL TECHNOLOGY

Application Process

Prospective Form One day students and Form Five Parents and students should apply via email. Form one boarders should apply on the site. Day Form1 students and Lower 6 send an email to takudzwabangira@gmail.com Lower 6 applications Applicants should apply online attach your form 4 results slips a copy of birth certificate. indicate your combination of three subjects you want to do at ‘A’ level. One can only choose subjects where they scored an A at ‘O’ level. The minimum number of A’s required to do sciences, commercials and arts are 9, 7 and 5 respectively. The minimum number is however subject to change depending on the general performance of the students for each particular year. About 148 vacancies exist for students . Preference is given to former but there are usually some places reserved for students who performed exceptionally well in other schools.
